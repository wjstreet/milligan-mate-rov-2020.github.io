About Us

Welcome to Milligan Robotics! We are an up and coming robotics team from Milligan University. We are exited and eager to compete in robotics competitions and to serve our regional community with our team's shared passion for making things.

Our Mission:

Milligan Robotics seeks to honor God as a team of servant leaders by addressing environmental and humanitarian challenges through the exploration and development of robotics applications.
